This is to inform the general public thta application into 76 Regular Course of The Nigerian Defence Academy (NDA) is Ongoing. Application into the NDA is open to both Male and Female Nigerians.

All Interested candidatesare expected to Log on to the Portal www.ndaapplications.net and pay for their application remita in th sum of N3,500 only. Interested candidates are advised to download and read the admission requirements carefully.

NOTE: Applicants applying for Admission into NDA must also apply to the Year 2024 Joint Admission Matriculation Board (JAMB) and must select NDA as first choice. Such applicants are expected to sit for the JAMB year 2024 Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) and obtain a minimum score of 180 for all courses except for Egineering courses where candidates must score a minimum of 210.

Also Note that requirements for undergraduate degree programmes and all other information concerning degree courses are detailed in the NDA website www.nda.edu.ng. Application for Admission into NDA will close on the 30TH April 2024. Only candidates who obtain the minimum JAMB(UTME) score for NDA will be invited to select a centre and sit for the NDA Screening Test. The test will be conducted at various centres across the nation on Saturday 25 May, 2024.

Successfull candidates from the NDA Screening Test will subsequently be invited for the Armed Forces Selection Board (AFSB) at NDA for detailed medical physical and mental health test following which the list of admitted candidates will be published.

For reference materials, a compenduim of Past NDA Screening test Questions and Answers can be obtained from the Academy for a token fee.
